APPLICATION PROCESSING

Applying for an examination is the first touch point for a potential candidate. The traditional application procedure is tedious, error prone and causes delay in the process and is highly inconvenient for stakeholders.
As the scope of internet increases, many examination bodies have begun leveraging technology to extend internet based services to their test takers. MeritTrac with a rich history of supporting large scale application processing for high stake examinations, has helped many examination bodies transform from pen and paper applications to OMR applications and now on to Computer based mode.
Online Application System
Online applications are accessible from virtually anywhere, and provide the convenience to fill in the application form at any time. Candidates create a login account for themselves in order to access the online form, for the first time. During subsequent logins, they fill in the necessary details, and submit the form. Post submission, they can at any time access their account, to either view the status of their application form, or even to download the e-Hall Tickets, once they are generated.
Skillfarms designs and hosts an end-to-end Online Application System, for the examination body. This includes:
Online Application form
Online Payment gateway
Online Test Booking System
The solution is hosted in a secure data centre with a high uptime. Applicant data as entered by the Applicant would be captured online as per the rules defined and data will be accessible only to authorized personnel. All data pertaining to the Online Application system may be downloaded in an Excel sheet for use. The application would have two interfaces accessible through separate logins – one for applicants and another for authorized executives of the examination body. There will be a facility to upload a photo and scanned signature. The online forms will auto generate a unique application number. The system short lists candidates based on the rules defined by the examination body

UST-IN-TIME QUESTION PAPER (JIT – QP)

The increasing population and Gross Enrolment Ratio make examinations large in India. This has brought examinations under constant threat resulting in inconvenience to students and dent to the examination body’s reputation. Skillfarms Just in Time Question Paper Management (JIT-QP) is designed to mitigate the risks in examination.
This is designed to secure examinations from vulnerabilities arising at Question Paper development, printing, logistics and storage before it reaches the test taker. JIT-QP ensures that the final question paper is decided randomly from a set of questions based on pre-defined rules. The final question paper is selected and made available at the exam centre in an encrypted mode just few hours before the exam.
The Process In today’s generation, it is important to leverage technology in the examination process that eliminates possibilities of paper leaks, delays and errors in results. Skillfarms Just in Time Question Paper Management (JIT-QP) is designed to addresses this very specific requirement.
Process 1: Highly secured and monitored facilities authored by Subject Matter Experts
Process 2: Multiple sets of question papers created by the Question Bank Manager
Process 3: Uploaded data securely stored giving access to the Controller of Examination or authorized personnel
Process 4: Final question paper to be selected by the Controller of Examination and encrypted in Question packs
Process 5: Sending passwords to pre-registered mobile numbers of authorized representatives in test centres
Process 6: Accessing the Question Pack with the password and printing them according to the classroom and seat allocation
Process 7: The printed papers are packed classroom wise and are available only at the time of examination
Process 8: Under surveillance room to access question papers and its printing

COMPUTER BASED TEST

Education across the country has transformed by leveraging and applying technology in almost all aspects. Teaching and learning methods have drastically changed over the years and the advent of technology has fostered this transformation.
Skillfarms, being a pioneer in the new age examination process in the country, has been supporting organizations to help them modernize their examination processes with the use of technology. We have supported many large and prestigious institutions, to help them transform from the Pen and Paper mode of delivery to Computer Based Tests (CBT). CBTs are increasingly being adopted these days for most of the high stake examinations. Since the beginning of this decade, over 100 large examinations in the country have migrated to computer based exams.
End-to-end Support
We provide end-to-end support for computer based exams from application processing to result processing. Enhanced prestige of the examination body by using next generation technologies

Instant score generation for faster rank list development
Convenience to candidates by giving them the option to choose examination dates and schedules from among multiple batches
